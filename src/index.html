UniBuddy University Timetable Planner

Just a few simple steps

Use this tool to find the ideal timetable for next semester

  1. Choose which semester you're planning for
  2. Choose your topics
  3. Order your criteria for an ideal table (maximize number of days free, minimize time spent at university, etc.)
  4. Click "Generate Timetables" and choose your ideal timetable

Spread the word

UniBuddy is run for free by a handful of students from Flinders University. All that we get out of making UniBuddy is the satisfaction that we're helping people.

If you find this tool useful, your friends probably will too, so please thank us by sharing it with your friends :)

If you find a bug or have other feedback, let us know on our Facebook page here.

Made by Tobias Wooldridge (email me) with contributions from Russell Peake. Want to help develop UniBuddy? It's open source!

While we try our best to be right, there's always a chance that we get things wrong about when classes are and whether they're full or not.

Be aware that you may not be able to get your ideal timetable even if the timetable planner says a class is not full. If there are issues, contact us on Facebook.
